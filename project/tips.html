Travel Tips

  • Language: Learn basic Portuguese phrases to connect with locals.
  • Food: Sample feijoada, pão de queijo, brigadeiro, and açaí.
  • Transportation: Use apps like Uber or public buses. Metro is great in large cities.
  • Safety: Avoid isolated areas, stay alert in busy zones, and don’t flash valuables.
  • Weather: Pack light for coastal regions and warm clothing for mountain towns.
