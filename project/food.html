Delicious Brazilian Food

  • Feijoada: A hearty stew made with black beans and various meats, traditionally served with rice, orange slices, and farofa.
  • Pão de Queijo: Chewy cheese bread balls made from cassava flour and cheese — a snack or breakfast favorite.
  • Açaí: A purple Amazonian fruit served frozen and topped with granola and banana — refreshing and energizing!
  • Brigadeiro: A classic Brazilian chocolate truffle made from condensed milk, cocoa powder, and sprinkles.
  • Moqueca: A fish stew made with coconut milk, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and cilantro — especially popular in Bahia.
