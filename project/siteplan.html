Explore Brazil: Website Planning Document

Site Name

Explore Brazil

This name represents an interactive travel guide designed for first-time visitors to Brazil. It reflects the idea of adventure, discovery, and cultural exploration.

Site Purpose

The site introduces Brazil’s most iconic destinations, including Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, and the Amazon Rainforest. It offers practical travel tips on culture, food, safety, and transportation. Users can explore images, weather updates, and create a personalized travel wishlist.

Scenarios

  • What are the must-visit places in Brazil for a first-time traveler?
  • How do I get around Brazil safely and efficiently as a tourist?

Color Schema

  • #009739 (Brazil Green) – Used for headings and accents to represent Brazil's forests and nature.
  • #ffcc29 (Brazil Yellow) – Used for borders and highlight areas, symbolizing Brazil’s vibrancy and flag.
  • #002776 (Brazil Blue) – Used for body text and occasional backgrounds to evoke calm and harmony.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for main headings to convey elegance and distinction.
  • Roboto – Used for body text and paragraphs to ensure clarity and readability.
